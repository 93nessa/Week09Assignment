Easy types of indoor plants..



Swiss Cheese Plant

The Swiss cheese plant (Monstera adansonii) gets its unique name from the fenestration on its leaves. As they resemble Swiss Cheese. It is a tropical plant that is fast growing and vining.


Mican Philodendron

Micans are known for their velvety, heart-shaped leaves. It is low maintenance and easy to grow. It is native to the tropical regions of Mexico and the Caribbean.
