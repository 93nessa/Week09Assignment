Tips


Soil
One of the most important components to plants is their soil. Always make sure your soil is breathable and can let water flow to the whole pot. You can use store bought soil or mix your own. You can use perlite, coco coir, orchid bark, and poting soil for this. Even lava rocks.
Lighting

The amount of light a plant gets is crucial to their growth. Make sure they can get natural light if possible. Otherwise use a grow light close to your plants.

Make sure you research the type of plant you have and how much light they need. Some might need full light so you can place close to the window. Others have sensitive leaves so you have to place them a couple of feet away from a window.

Potting
The type of pot also plays an important role for the plant. Depending on its root system. Some plants like to be confined into their pots, so they need to have extra space. Others need shallow pots due to having a small root system. Having an indaquate pot can cause root rot.
Watering
The most important to plant care is watering. Your watering schedule should change depending on the season and also the plant. Some plants require frequent watering. While others only need water once or twice a month.

Tricks


Rocks
Adding rocks to soil can help it breathe and are great for drainage. Some types to use are lava rocks, perlite, granite, and lecca.
Humidifier

Plants love humidity. A good way to keep the humidity up is with a humidifier. Make sure NOT to add any oils into it.

Grow Lights
Grow lights are a great way to control the amout of light your plants get. You can place it close to your plants and watch them flurish.
